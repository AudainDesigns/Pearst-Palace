MRSHE PALACE

HISTORY

Our holiday residence is situated in the middle of a little medieval town Perast, once a prosperous navy centre whose 300 houses were built in the 17th and 18th century. Today Perast is protected as a World Heritage Site in an area of outstanding natural beauty and dramatic scenery.

The house was a home of a well-known merchant family Mrshe for over 2 centuries and was classified as a ruin in the land registry in the beginning of the last century.

We spent the best part of two years completely restoring it and trying to recreate its former glory, by using only natural material of stone and wood and keeping the original generous dimensions of every room in the house.

The house covers more than 7000sq feet and is equipped with modern amenities throughout including flat screen TV, DVD, iPod docks, wifi and air-conditioning. It also has a completely walled and private spacious front courtyard and terraced back gardens and is only 2 mins or 62 steps away from the sea, which is crystal clear and warm in the summer months.

Mrshe Palace accomodates up to 22 people very comfortably. In addition to this we can organize car hire, daily cooking / cleaning / babysitting, hiring a crewed boat for daily trips. The palace has also become a popular wedding venue, which we can also help to organise.
