ACCOMODATION

The residence is situated in the middle of medieival Perast, an area of outstanding natural beauty. The house was a home of a well-known merchant family Mrshe for over 2 centuries.

Mrshe Palace accomodates up to 22 people very comfortably. In addition to this we can organize car hire, daily cooking / cleaning / babysitting, hiring a crewed boat for daily trips. The palace has also become a popular conference and wedding venue, which we can also help to organise - offering full hotel-style service.

The house covers more than 7000sq feet and is equipped with modern amenities throughout including flat screen TV, DVD, iPod docks, wifi and air-conditioning. It also has a completely walled and private spacious front courtyard and terraced back gardens and is only 2 mins or 62 steps away from the sea, which is crystal clear and warm in the summer months.
